Why Choose Particular About Trim?

We're a caring and dependable family company, owned and operated since 1987 in Bexley, OH. We use only Particular About Trim employees, who are insured and fully covered by Worker's Compensation. We do not use subcontractors.

We're passionate about what we do and we keep current with industry advances in product development and techniques. We've built a well-respected business around our personal and professional expectations, eagerly sharing our skill, our enthusiasm, and our neighborly rapport with local homeowners.

We use premium Benjamin Moore Paints, because our customers deserve performance, durability, range of color, as well as low odor and low VOC.

And finally, we are particular about your trim - as evidenced by our thorough preparation of your home's surfaces, our crisply painted lines, our complete coverage, and our conscientious care and protection of your home's indoor floors and furnishings and your outdoor paved areas and landscaping.
